Trip Summary

A 5-day self-drive adventure for you, your wife, and daughters (11 & 17) from Bangalore (Oct 18) through Mysore's heritage, Ooty's misty hills (2 nights, Oct 18-20), to Kerala's Cherai Beach and Alleppey backwaters (Oct 21-22). Focus on new discoveries, cultural immersion, photography ops, nature trails, kid-friendly activities like boating & water sports, and savoring local cuisine. Total ~900km driving. October weather: Mild 20-30°C, occasional showers—pack light layers & rain gear. Return to Bangalore Oct 24.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Sat Oct 18 - Bangalore to Ooty via Mysore (Drive: ~270km, 7hrs)

Start: 6AM | End: 8PM. Scenic drive through coffee plantations; brief heritage stop en route. Stay: Luxury resort like Savoy IHCL SeleQtions (scenic colonial charm).

  • 6AM-9AM: Depart Bangalore, drive to Mysore (140km, 3hrs).
  • 10AM-12PM: Mysore Palace (attraction below) . Duration: 2hrs.
  • 12PM-1PM: Lunch at Dasaprakash (local thali). Duration: 1hr.
  • 1PM-4:30PM: Drive to Ooty via Bandipur (130km, 3.5hrs; wildlife spotting)
  • 5PM-8PM: Check-in; evening stroll at Ooty Lake . Duration: 3hrs.

Weather Backup: Extend Mysore Palace (indoor); Ooty Government Museum.

Day 2: Sun Oct 19 - Ooty Nature & Boating

Start: 7AM | End: 9PM. Cool climes; gardens & lake for family fun. Stay: Same resort.

  • 7AM-10AM: Government Botanical Garden (attraction below) . Duration: 3hrs.
  • 10:30AM-12PM: Ooty Lake boating (pedal boats INR 300/30min) . Duration: 1.5hrs.
  • 12PM-1PM: Lunch at local cafe (Nilgiri tea & pakoras).
  • 1PM-4PM: Doddabetta Peak (viewpoint, photography) . Duration: 3hrs.
  • Evening: Relax, tea tasting.

Weather Backup: Indoor Chocolate Museum; cafe hopping.

Day 3: Mon Oct 20 - Ooty Hills & Train

Start: 8AM | End: 8PM. Train & estates for exploration.

  • 8AM-12PM: Nilgiri Toy Train (Ooty-Coonoor, 1.5hr; options: Regular INR 100, First Class INR 180; book IRCTC). Taxi back. Duration: 4hrs.
  • 12:30PM-2PM: Sims Park (attraction below) . Duration: 1.5hrs.
  • 2PM-3PM: Lunch at local spot.
  • 3PM-6PM: Tea Estates photography (e.g., near Coonoor, guided walk INR 500). Duration: 3hrs.
  • Evening: Resort unwind.

Weather Backup: Ooty Art Gallery; indoor tea factory tour.

Day 4: Tue Oct 21 - Ooty to Cherai Beach (Drive: 350km, 8hrs)

Start: 9AM | End: 7PM. Descent to coast; beach unwind. Stay: Cherai Beach Resorts (beachfront luxury).

  • 9AM-10AM: Quick revisit Doddabetta if missed. Duration: 1hr.
  • 10AM-6PM: Drive to Cherai (highways, tea stops). Google Maps: Route.
  • 6PM-7PM: Sunset walk at Cherai Beach (dolphin spotting). Duration: 1hr.
  • Evening: Dinner at Chilliout Cafe (seafood).

Weather Backup: Kochi spice market if rain-delayed.

Day 5: Wed Oct 22 - Cherai & Alleppey Backwaters

Start: 8AM | End: Overnight. Beach fun & houseboat. Stay: Private houseboat (1 night). Return drive Thu/Fri.

  • 6PM-7PM: Sunset walk at Cherai Beach (dolphin spotting) . Duration: 1hr.
  • 12PM-1:30PM: Lunch at Lilliput (Kerala seafood).
  • 2PM-3PM: Drive to Alleppey (60km, 1hr). Google Maps: Route.
  • 3PM-Onward: Board private houseboat (2BR luxury, AC, meals; INR 20,000). Cruise backwaters, birdwatching. Overnight anchor.

Weather Backup: Indoor cooking class; Alleppey museum.

Houseboat Options: Private (INR 20,000 family); Sharing (INR 12,500/couple, but private for privacy). Luxury resorts: Veena by the Beach near Cherai.
