Practical Info

Money & Budget

The local currency is the Czech Koruna (CZK). While cards are widely accepted, especially in cities, it's good to have some cash for smaller purchases. Tipping is customary, usually around 10% for good service.

Phones & Connectivity

Most hotels, cafes, and restaurants offer free Wi-Fi. A local SIM card from a provider like Vodafone or O2 can be bought for cheap for data access on the go.

Season & Weather (September)

September is a fantastic time to visit. The summer crowds are gone, the weather is mild and pleasant, and the beginning of autumn brings beautiful colors. Pack layers as mornings and evenings can be cool. There is a chance of rain, so a light jacket or umbrella is a must.

Local Events

September is a great month for festivals. Check local listings for the Dvorak Prague Festival, the Prague Castle Wine Festival, and the St. Wenceslas Celebrations in ƒåesk√Ω Krumlov. [Source: PragueGO & VisitCzechia, checked Sep 22, 2025].
