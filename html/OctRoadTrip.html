Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Sat Oct 18 - Bangalore to Ooty via Mysore

Start: 6AM from Bangalore | End: Noon in Ooty. Drive through scenic routes, pass Mysore. Stay: NorsuStays–Near Rosegarden-View of RaceCourse&lake.

  • Mysore Expressway

  • Forest Drive Drive to Ooty via Bandipur (130km, 3.5hrs; wildlife spotting)

    • Noon: Check-in at NorsuStays–Near Rosegarden-View of RaceCourse&lake.
      • Airbnb Accomodation at a scenic location
  • Ooty Lake Check-in; evening stroll at Ooty Lake . Duration: 3hrs.
  • Government Botanical Garden (attraction below) . Duration: 3hrs.

    • Weather Backup: Ooty Government Museum.

    Practical Info

    Season: October (post-monsoon, pleasant; Ooty cool 10-21°C, Kerala warm 25-30°C with possible rain).

    Packing Checklist: Light clothes for Kerala, warm for Ooty, rain gear, comfortable shoes, camera, sunscreen, insect repellent, medications, ID, cash/ATM cards, power bank, snacks.

    Money: ATMs available; carry cash for remote areas. Phones: Good coverage; international roaming if needed.
